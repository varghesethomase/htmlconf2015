About Us

A non-profit association of front end enthusiasts in Kerala is bringing you the first HTML5/JS Developer Conference in the region. Handpicked speakers are invited to take sessions on interesting topics. A nominal registration fee is implied to help with the organization cost as well as to filter out no-shows. Special discounted rates are available for students. Free entry is available for financially under-privileged students. To avail entry under this scheme, please contact us at hello@html5conf.in (Information regarding those who avail the free passes will be kept private). Free media passes are also available, contact us for details.
